Occupancy Forecasting for Real Estate

Forecasting property occupancy using historical data, macroeconomic indicators, and visualization dashboards to support strategic decisions.

Overview

The goal of this project was to predict occupancy rates across a portfolio of properties and help the team understand how changes in the economy might affect vacancy and revenue.

Data & Tools

Combined property-level historical occupancy with external data such as unemployment rates, median income, and other macro indicators.

Tools: Python, Pandas, scikit-learn, Tableau / Power BI, Excel

Approach

  • Cleaned and joined multiple data sources into a single analysis-ready dataset.
  • Explored relationships between occupancy and external economic indicators.
  • Built and compared baseline models (e.g., regression, time-series approaches).
  • Visualized forecasts and scenarios in an interactive dashboard for stakeholders.

Results & Impact

  • Identified key drivers of occupancy and periods of higher vacancy risk.
  • Created forecasts that helped support planning around revenue and budgeting.
  • Delivered a dashboard where non-technical users could explore scenarios.
